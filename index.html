My Latest Work
The Winsters Rebrand

Transforming dated private-label packaging into a clean experience.

The Story

To help Winster Investment compete in wholesale markets, I led a full redesign of their food packaging, by transitioning outdated labels into a cohesive, modern system. Drawing inspiration from contemporary grocery trends, I refined layout, typography, and color to boost clarity and shelf presence while standardizing dielines for production efficiency. The result was a clean, scalable visual system that enhanced brand trust and simplified vendor workflows.

Scratch App

A youth-focused interface that brings convenient to  pick-up sports.

The Story

SCRATCH is a mobile app connecting users to local pick-up games, designed for a 10–29 age group. I used sports psychology insights and research to create an intuitive, energetic UI—using flat design and a bold orange palette to evoke movement and friendliness. The final product delivered a cross-platform UI kit that tested strongly for usability and engagement with intended users.

Lyra Font

Created with a look that's sharp, layered, and unmistakably sci-fi.

The Story

LYRA is a conceptual dual-layered typeface made for futuristic branding and entertainment media. I built it from the geometric foundations of Futura and the neutrality of Helvetica, introducing angular cuts, boxy edges, and layered neon strokes to evoke digital circuitry and cyberpunk themes. Built for both screen and print, LYRA delivers bold visual impact while remaining versatile and legible across formats.

Nissin Modern Organic Udon

Elevating a nostalgic staple with an eco-conscious, minimalist identity.

The Story

For Nissin’s entry into the premium organic market, I developed a sub-brand identity that appealed to younger, health-focused audiences without losing its value-driven roots. The design leaned into Whole Foods-style branding, muted tones, clean typography, and eco-conscious cues, striking a balance between affordability and modern minimalism. The resulting packaging system launched across print, web, and social, increasing visibility with the target demographic.
