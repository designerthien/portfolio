Summary

LYRA is a conceptual typeface crafted for entertainment and editorial use, especially in science fiction. The aim was to develop letter forms that scalable and strong in both print and digital environments.

Approach

Drawing inspiration from Futura's geometry and Helvetica's neutrality, LYRA adds sharp angles, squared counters, and dual-layered lines that mimic neon and circuitry.

Result

LYRA performs best in titles, posters, and cinematic branding. It's been tested in mockups for media assets, where it maintains clarity and a strong visual voice.

Summary

Nissin sought to break into the organic instant food space to rival premium brands like Mike's Mighty Good. I created a sub-brand identity, including packaging and digital assets, that appealed to younger, health-focused audiences without losing its value-driven roots.

Approach

The design drew from contemporary grocery branding, using muted tones, clean layouts, and eco-friendly packaging elements. The goal was to convey sustainability and simplicity while keeping the product approachable and affordable.
